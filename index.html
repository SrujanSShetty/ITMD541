About Course

This course focuses on helping students enhance their foundational knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It provides a comprehensive understanding of front-end web development and how these technologies work together to build interactive and responsive web applications.

Throughout the course, students will work on practical projects that will improve their coding skills and give them the confidence to tackle more complex front-end challenges. The emphasis is on building real-world applications, making it highly practical and project-based.

By the end of the course, students will be able to create dynamic websites, integrate different web components, and understand the core principles of web development.

Professor Brian Bailey is guiding the students with his extensive experience in web development and interactive design, ensuring that each participant gets a thorough and engaging learning experience.
